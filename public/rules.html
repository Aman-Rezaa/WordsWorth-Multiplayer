Game Rules

Objective

Guess the hidden word before you run out of time/tries.

How to Play

  • You have six attempts to guess a five-letter word.
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Tile Colors

  • Green: The letter is in the correct spot.
  • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Grey: The letter is not in the word at all.

Game Modes

  • Easy: 10 minutes to guess the word.
  • Medium: 5 minutes to guess the word.
  • Hard: 2.5 minutes to guess the word.
  • Insane: 1 minute to guess the word.

Special Features

  • Play Against PC: Compete against the computer.
  • Play Against Friends: Challenge your friends in a multiplayer mode.
  • Time Rush: Race against the clock in different difficulty modes.

Scoring

  • In single-player mode, player has no time limit and wins on a correct guess.
  • In time-rush mode, player wins on a correct guess before time runs out.
  • In multiplayer mode, scores are based on the time taken to guess the word correctly.
  • If the time runs out, the player is marked as "LOSER."
  • Player is marked as "CHEATER" if found cheating, process is abstracted for fair play.
  • Number of tries to arrive on a correct guess is 6 for all the modes.

Leaderboard

  • The leaderboard displays the top players and their times and can be accessed with the same game link.
  • The correct word and the final leaderboard will only be displayed after the contest ends despite the immediate win.
  • Winners are highlighted with a gold, silver and bronze medal.
  • Losers are listed at the middle.
  • Cheaters are listed at the bottom.
